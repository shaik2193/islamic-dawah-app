The Five Pillars of Islam

1

Shahada

Declaration of faith: "There is no god but Allah, and Muhammad is His messenger."

2

Salah

Prayer five times daily - a direct connection between the believer and Allah.

3

Zakat

Obligatory charity - purifying wealth by giving to those in need.

4

Sawm

Fasting during Ramadan - spiritual purification and self-discipline.

5

Hajj

Pilgrimage to Mecca - once in a lifetime for those who are able.
